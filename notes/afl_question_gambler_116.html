AfL Question Gambler



A way of adding fun and competition when students complete questions:



1) Allocate the amount of "Starting Cash" groups will be given.


2) Decide if money will be given as a "New Round Bonus" - using this prevents any group from running out of money.


3) Click "Distribute Starting Cash".


4) Ask students how much they would like to "Wager" on the next question - enter these values.


5) Identify which groups have the "Correct" answer - then click "Update Funds".


You can continue to repeat steps 4 and 5 until you have finished all of the questions.
