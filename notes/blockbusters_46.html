Blockbusters


When you select Blockbusters, you are greeted by this screen:



LOAD QUESTIONS


Click here to find out how to create a bank of questions and load them into the game.


To play:


When you have a bank of questions loaded, simply click on the letter to reveal the question (and the answer) then select the colour you want for the cell:



Point to note:

The Blockbusters game reveals the first letter of the answer, for example:


Beginning with "T": What is the name of this software?


This means the answer to each question must begin with a normal letter, not with a number.


If you wanted to use the question:

Q=What is 2 + 2?


You would need to spell out the answer "four" rather than use the answer "4":

A=Four


So the Blockbusters game would display:


Beginning with "F": What is 2 + 2?
