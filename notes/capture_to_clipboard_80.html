Capture to Clipboard

(Shortcut WindowsKey and CTRL and Left Drag)



The ability to capture a portion of the screen to use in your lesson has become very useful.


You can do this using the menu option, or by holding down two keys:

WindowsKey and CTRL key

While these are held down, CLICK your mouse and DRAG a rectangle over the image you would like to capture.


       ALTERNATE KEYS:

       Hold down the PrintScreen Key and CLICK your mouse and DRAG a rectangle.


The screen captured is instantly added to your clipboard:



To paste the captured image into you lesson you can either:


Right click and select PASTE

Use the menu to choose EDIT and the PASTE

Press CTRL and C
