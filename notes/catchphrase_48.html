CatchPhrase


When you select CatchPhrase, you will be greeted with this window:



First select a grid size, then load in an image.


LOAD QUESTIONS


Click here to find out how to create a bank of questions and load them into the game.


To play:


When you have a bank of questions loaded, click on the

NEXT QUESTION

button to reveal a question



The NEXT QUESTION button with change to an ANSWER button while the question is being displayed.


If the answer is correct, click on a number to remove it and reveal the image.
