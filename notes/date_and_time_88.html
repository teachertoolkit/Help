Date and Time


While the Teacher Toolkit is running - it keeps an eye on everything you type.


Open any document, and try typing the word "date" but as you type it add the symbol # somewhere in the middle. E.g. type: da#te


It will automatically replace your text with today's date.


Depending on where you place the # symbol, it will provide a different format, examples of the formats available are:


d#ate = 28Feb2005

da#te = 28/02/2005

dat#e = Monday 28 February 2005

date# = Monday the 28th of February 2005

t#ime = 8:24

ti#me = 8:24 AM

tim#e = 08:24:34


This was designed to give teachers a quick and easy way of adding date/times to lessons and documents.
