Example Changes


Samples of common renaming changes:


1) To remove everything between two brackets, e.g.


Exam Paper[June 2016].pdf

to

Exam Paper.pdf


Fill in the two BETWEEN boxes, but leave the WITH box empty:


Note, you may also want to remove a space e.g.

Exam Paper [June 2016] for test.pdf

to

Exam Paper for test.pdf


To do this, include a space before the first bracket in your BETWEEN box:


2) To replace something between brackets, and also keep the brackets - simply include brackets in the WITH section, e.g.

Exam Paper (June 2016).pdf

to

Exam Paper (to print).pdf


3) To number all the files, and leave a space after the number e.g.

Class test.doc

to

01 Class text.pdf
