Guideline for Reading

Also part of the On-Screen Toolbar


The guideline ensures everyone can follow what is on the board, it brings focus and attention to visual displays and allows students to quickly move to and from the board without losing their train-of-thought.




It sits just below the mouse and does not impair the use of your computer. It can be turned off by pressing the ESCAPE KEY or by deactivating it on the On-Screen Toolbar
