The Teacher Toolkit

The little program that produces big impacts.


PURPOSE:


The Teacher Toolkit was designed to give teachers instant access to a range of tools that will help them consistently deliver engaging, personalised, outstanding lessons.


It is important that the Teacher Toolkit is fast and very reliable.


The Teacher Toolkit can run over a network (allowing access to shared resources) or it can run straight from your USB Flash Drive, which means you can take it with you.
