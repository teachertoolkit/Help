Keep Window Active


This feature has many uses, from keeping lesson content displayed on the white-board while the teacher does the register, to keeping data displayed while it is utilised in another program.


The keep window active just makes the selected window remain displayed on top of any other window, even if you click off of it.


To use this feature, hold down the WindowsKey and click on the window you would like to keep active.

       ALTERNATE SHORTCUT:

       Hold down the ScrollLock Key and click on the window you would like to keep active


Now, when you click on any other window, it will not remove your active window from view.


To turn off this feature, repeat the process.
