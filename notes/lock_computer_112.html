Lock Your Computer

(Shortcut Key = CTRL and WindowsKey and Alt and L)


If you need to leave your computer unsupervised, you may want to lock it to prevent anyone accessing your files.


The Teacher Toolkit will lock the keyboard and mouse for you, preventing any unauthorised access.


To do this, press the keyboard shortcut and enter a password. When you return, simply re-type your password.


The keyboard shortcut requires holding the following three keys down:



and then pressing the letter L.


Note - if you forget your password, you will need to restart the computer.
