Meditation Alarm


Meditation can be a great way to de-stress and to focus your mind. To enable you to undertake a period of mediation, without worrying about the time, you can set the meditation alarm to:


Beep when your allocated time is over

OR

Shut off (and/or mute) your computer



Other features include the option to dim the brightness of the computer screen, and have messages read out to you. Note - this requires that you have a "Text to Speech" engine working on your computer.
