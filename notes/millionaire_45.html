Millionaire


When you choose this game, you are greeted with the following screen:



Down the right-hand side there are a selection of buttons:


Create Questions



Edit Questions


If you have a saved bank of questions, you can edit them and make alterations



Load Questions and Play


You can play the game by clicking your selected answer




Improvised Question Correct

AND

Improvised Question Incorrect


The options allow the teacher to override the normal game settings, for example if the teacher wants to set an impromptu question or wants to go through a multiple-choice exam paper.
