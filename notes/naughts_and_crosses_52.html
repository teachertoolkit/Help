Naughts and Crosses


When you select NAUGHTS AND CROSSES, you are greeted by the following screen:




LOAD QUESTIONS


Click here to find out how to create a bank of questions and load them into the game.


To play:


When you have a bank of questions loaded, click NEXT QUESTION to move through the questions and answers.


When a team answers correctly, click on the space to convert it to their symbol.


A variation to the game can be played by allowing players to STEAL BACK coordinates. To do this, simply click on the coordinate to scroll through the options.
