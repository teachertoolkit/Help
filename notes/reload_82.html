Reload

(Shortcut WindowsKey and R)

       (Alternate shortcut ScrollLock and R)


Sometimes schools are forced to make software updates or changes during the course of the day.


This can prevent other software from working correctly.


Usually, to rectify this, you are asked to restart you computer - which can be very inconvenient and spoil the flow of your lesson.


To ensure the Teacher Toolkit will always work, without being re-started, you can choose to refresh it.



This will instantly re-set everything and ensure the Teacher Toolkit works as though it has just been re-started.
